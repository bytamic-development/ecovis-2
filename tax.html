Tax

National and international tax advisory with a reginal focus and pragmatic solutions due to years of experience in various areas of taxation.

Efficient advice in all tax-related matters

Scope of services

Ongoing tax advice

  • Preparation of all kinds of tax returns
  • Representation against tax authorities (on an ongoing basis and in special matters)
  • Support in connection with tax compliance and accompanying processes
  • Business analysis and evaluations
  • Tax strategies and their implementation
  • Ongoing information on changes in taxation
  • Consulting in matters of payroll tax and social security
  • Analysis of tax structuring options
  • Tax planning and optimisation

  • Employee participation programmes
