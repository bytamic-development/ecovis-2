Customer Portal

Our customer portal is an efficient tool for digital accounting, controlling and planning. It offers the possibility of uploading documents of all kinds (incoming invoices, outgoing invoices, cash book, contracts, insurance policies, etc.) in all file formats via web browser and sending them to us. In addition, accounting data can be accessed from anywhere and at any time. This enables efficient further processing and we can provide evaluations from accounting prompt.
